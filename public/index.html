Rounds

Fish bowl has 3 rounds of game play where teams will take turns trying to guess as many of the words in the bowl as they can. Turns last 90 seconds and the team with the most phrases guessed at the end of the game wins!

The first round is Taboo where the clue giver can describe the phrase using any words except the words in the phrase.

The second round is Charades where the clue giver can act out the phrase. No words or sounds are allowed!

The third round is Password where the clue giver can use one word to describe the phrase. Choose wisely!

Turns

The game begins with one team being chosen randomly. One player on the team is assigned to be the clue giver (indicated by the "*") while the rest of the team are guessers. The clue giver switches to the next player on the team after each correctly guessed phrase.

The clock starts running once the first phrase is drawn during a team's turn. The clock continues to run so be ready to draw if you are next up!
